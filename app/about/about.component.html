About

Transparent, hassle free and affordable
shipping for your freight.

America's trucking industry is a $749 billion sector in need of disruption. GoShipNow eliminates the middlemen, connecting shippers directly with trucks. This will save shippers 20 - 45% and put more money in the pocket of drivers.

Empty trucks account for 20 billion miles driven each year in the United States. GoShipNow will utilize that empty space for shipping freight, making more money for drivers and further reducing the cost for shippers.

Often drivers find themselves waiting anywhere from 2 to 10 hours at the dock while warehouse workers load the truck. With our technology, we will provide the warehouse staff with real time arrival forecasts so that they have the freight ready to load once the truck arrives. These features will make everyone happier.
