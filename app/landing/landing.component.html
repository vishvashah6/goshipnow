How it works
1

Give us the details

Fill in the freight details.
Provide us with the length, width, height, weight & type of shipment.
Let us know the pickup and drop-off location.
Tell us when you want us to pick it up.

2

See the cost and post

Our algorithm instantly calculates the cost.
Its based upon shipment size, load class and distance.
If you like the cost, click POST!

3

Get matched

Our system takes only a few seconds to find a truck.
It starts off by looking for trucks already headed in your direction.
It narrows it down to only those trucks having enough space.
This way you only pay for the space you use.

4

Pickup

Once you're matched, the driver get notified.
You can track the location of the truck using our app or website.
Once the truck arrives,
the driver picks up the goods and
drives to the destination.

5

Delivery

The shipment gets delivered.
You get a notification.
Everyone is happy.
